Privacy Policy

Last updated January 2026

OILIVE ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect on this website and how we use it.

What we collect

Currently, the only personal information we collect is the email address you voluntarily submit through our "Notify Me" form. We also use privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics (page views and general traffic patterns) to understand how visitors use the site — this does not identify you personally.

How we use it

  • To notify you when OILIVE products become available for purchase.
  • To occasionally share news about our harvest, launch, or brand — never more than a few emails a year.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data storage

Email addresses are stored securely with our email service provider. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from any email at any time using the link provided in that email, or by contacting us directly. If you are a resident of the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights under GDPR/CCPA, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data.
